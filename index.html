Meet Our Founder

Mr. Ashok Kumar Ghosh, the visionary behind Jaya Construction, believes a home isn't built with bricks—it's built with heart. With over 25 years of experience crafting landmark properties in Kolkata, his philosophy blends tradition with innovation to create more than spaces—he creates legacies.

"We don't just build homes. We craft experiences. We build dreams."

The Legacy of Leadership

1999 – The Beginning

Mr. Ashok Kumar Ghosh laid the foundation of Jaya Construction with a vision to redefine urban living in Kolkata.

2006 – First Landmark Project

Completed 'Skyline Residency' in Jodhpur Park — a project that set new standards in luxury living.

2012 – Expansion to Santoshpur

Ventured into Santoshpur with the prestigious 'Jaya Heights' and introduced smart apartment concepts.

2018 – 1000+ Homes Delivered

Marked a major milestone by successfully handing over more than 1000 residential units with 99% client satisfaction.

2025 – Future-Ready Vision

Leading the charge towards sustainable, AI-powered luxury homes with a focus on community well-being and modernity.
